Make Block Final

The Make block robot final was a great success. Along with the Itchy Itchiford team we created a soccer beast. The main goal of the robot was to have folding wings that could be sent out and in for out choosing. We also had to modify the robot's original design. We made it smaller, taller and wider. This helped in movement and stability whilst driving. We also raised the brain and battery to give room for the wings.
